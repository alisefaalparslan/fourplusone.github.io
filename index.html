What is 4+1

4+1 is a new health club concept that is designed for everybody – we want to make Turkey a fitter place.

We aim to provide a great range of high quality facilities in a fun and exciting environment and all at great value membership prices.

Whether you are an experienced gym user or this is your first time joining a health club, you will find everything you need at a 4+1 health club.
